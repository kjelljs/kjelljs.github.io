Kjell Johan edits

Since October 2019, Louise McNally and I have been editors-in-chief of Semantics and Pragmatics, a journal of the Linguistic Society of America and the only one of the four main semantics journals to be open access.

Louise and I strive to sustain the successes achieved by the founding editors, David Beaver and Kai von Fintel, maintaining the exceptional standards set by them for the quality and timeliness of the editorial process.
