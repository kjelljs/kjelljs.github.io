Empty nouns (ENs) and NP ellipsis (NPE) have different resolution conditions, and maybe different depths.

EN vs. NPE in (say) German

(1) and (2) differ in the requirements they place on contexts they could be used in.

  1. Geben Sie mir das Teuerste, was Sie haben.
  2. Geben Sie mir den teuersten, den Sie haben.

How?

The pair would not work in English. It would in Norwegian (Jeg skal få det/den dyreste dere har), but the German one features two extra differences, over and above gender…
