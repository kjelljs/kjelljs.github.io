--- layout: post title: "Two letters on language" ---

So graciously, Claudio Magris wrote back when in a letter I expressed my excitement over a guest lecture he held in Oslo in the fall of 1976.

A key sentence: "Mich auch interessiert außerordentlich das Verhältnis zwischen der Struktur des Satzes und jener der Wirklichkeit."

English: I, too, am fascinated by the relationship between the structure of the sentence and that of reality."
